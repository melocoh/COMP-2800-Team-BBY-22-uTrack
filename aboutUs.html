uTrack

uTrack is a community-driven supply tracking web application designed to inform users of the availability of essential items during CoViD-19.

Our Mission

Information regarding supply stocks is provided by the users who go out of their way to report the current status of items they find while shopping.
The more they post and contribute to their local community, the more points they will accumulate, which can be redeemed for in-app cosmetics.
Associated items on demand pertain to hygienic, clinical, and dietary needs such as toilet paper, diapers, and hand sanitizers.
